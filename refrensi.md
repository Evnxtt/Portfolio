## 🎯 Vision & Mission

### Our Vision 👀
Creating graduates who are competent according to industry needs with character, religious, professional, and environmental awareness.

### Our Mission 🧭
1. Creating a religious school community through education that is loaded with imtaq (Islamic spirituality)
2. Building student discipline behavior from an early age
3. Improving student abilities in intracurricular and extracurricular activities
4. Improving the effectiveness of teaching and learning activities (KBM) and guidance for all school members in enforcing discipline based on willingness and full responsibility
5. Developing and encouraging professionalism in work, both for teachers and administrative staff to obtain quality human resources
6. Preparing graduates to be absorbed by Industry, capable of entrepreneurship, and able to continue their studies to higher levels
7. Creating a green, beautiful, safe, and comfortable school environment

## 🚀 Installation

1. **Clone the repository**:
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/your-username/BPPI.git
   ```

2. **Set up your local server** (XAMPP, WAMP, etc.)

3. **Import the database**:
   - Import `bppi_sch.sql` to your MySQL database
   - Create a database named `bppi_sch`

4. **Configure database connection** in `koneksi.php`:
   ```php
   $koneksi = mysqli_connect("localhost", "root", "", "bppi_sch");
   ```

5. **Run the application**:
   - Access the website through your local server
   - Default admin credentials: username: `admin`, password: `admin`
   - Default operator credentials: username: `operator`, password: `operator`
